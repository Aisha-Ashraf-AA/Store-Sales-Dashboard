Store Sales Dashboard

Project Overview

This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of retail sales performance from 2014 to 2017. It is designed to track key performance indicators (KPIs), identify top-performing customers and product categories, and visualize geographical sales distribution across the United States.

Key Features & Visualizations

•	Executive Summary KPIs: High-level overview of Total Sales ($2.3M), Total Quantity (38K), and Total Profit ($286K).
•	Geospatial Analysis: A map visualization showing sales distribution by state, allowing for regional performance tracking.
•	Customer Insights: A "Top 5 Customers by Profit" chart identifying high-value clients like Tamara Chand and Raymond Buch.
•	Product Hierarchy: Analysis of sales by sub-category (e.g., Phones and Chairs leading the market) and profit trends across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology.
•	Temporal Trends: * Sales by Year: Showing consistent year-over-year growth, peaking at $0.73M in 2017.
o	Monthly Sales Trend: An area chart highlighting seasonal fluctuations and peak sales periods in the fourth quarter.
Technical Stack
•	Tool: Power BI 

•	Data Modeling: Implementation of time-series calculations and category groupings.
•	DAX/Calculations: Used for dynamic KPIs and year-over-year growth metrics.

Key Business Insights

1.	Growth Trajectory: Sales have shown a steady upward trend from 2014 ($0.48M) to 2017 ($0.73M), indicating a healthy expanding market.
2.	Category Performance: Technology and Office Supplies consistently outperform Furniture in terms of profit margins.
3.	Seasonality: There is a significant spike in sales activity starting in September and peaking in November/December, likely driven by holiday demand.
4.	Product Focus: Phones and Chairs are the primary revenue drivers, contributing over $650K combined to total sales.
